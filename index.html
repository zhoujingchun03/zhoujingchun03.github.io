Biography

I received M.S. degree in software engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), Beijing, China, in 2016. I then earned another M.S. degree in computer information technology from Northern Arizona University (NAU), Flagstaff, AZ, USA, in 2022, and a Ph.D. degree in computer applications from Dalian Maritime University (DMU), Dalian, China, in 2021. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Dalian Maritime University (DMU) and a visiting fellow at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), China. I serve as a reviewer for journals and conferences including IEEE TIP, TGRS, SPL, JOE, AI, EAAI, Information Fusion, AMM, CEA, SPIC, Neurocomputing, Journal of Shanghai Jiao Tong University (Science), OE, ACCV, and more. My research interests encompass computer vision, deep learning, and underwater image enhancement.
